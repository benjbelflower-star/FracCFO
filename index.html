5 Finance Mistakes That Sink Seasonal Operators

  • Treating cash flow as a monthly problem instead of weekly
  • Overestimating peak season revenue
  • Underestimating off-season expenses
  • Failing to run scenario models
  • Ignoring timing of inventory and prepaid expenses

Simple Cash Flow Forecast for Seasonal Businesses

  • Review 2-3 years of historical revenue to identify patterns
  • Separate fixed from variable expenses
  • Adjust for seasonal trends
  • Build a rolling 13-week forecast
  • Update weekly during high-risk periods

3 Scenarios Every Operator Should Model Before Expanding

  • Best Case: Demand exceeds expectations
  • Base Case: Performance matches forecast
  • Worst Case: Revenue 20% below plan with higher costs
  • Evaluate impact on cash, staffing, and margins
  • Only expand when worst case is survivable

How I Consolidated 180 Profit Centers Into 14

  • Identified reporting overload and decision fatigue
  • Mapped profit centers into logical operating groups
  • Standardized the chart of accounts
  • Built KPI dashboards for each group
  • Reduced reporting time by 40% while improving clarity
